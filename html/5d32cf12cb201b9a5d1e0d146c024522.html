Wanna know what Larry Merchant WON'T be doing on Saturday night?

Watching Floyd Mayweather fight Andre Berto ... 'cause the legendary boxing commentator says that any Mayweather fight that doesn't involve GGG is a total waste of time.

Merchant was out in L.A. recently when we, of course, had to ask for his expert opinion on the huge fight (with the not so huge buzz) in Vegas this weekend.
