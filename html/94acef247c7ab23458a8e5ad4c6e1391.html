16 new officers join Bayonne Police Department

  • Updated: Dec. 22, 2023, 1:59 p.m.|
  • Published: Dec. 22, 2023, 1:54 p.m.
Subscribers can gift articles to anyone
By
  • Margaret Schmidt | The Jersey Journal

Sixteen new officers have joined the Bayonne Police Department.

Adam Podwysocki, Jordan Quilca, James Ashe, Dayne Sabo, Nicholas Konopka, Edwin Zorrilla-Poueriet, Jean Candelaria, Mark Dworzanski, Christian Cella, Ramoncito Mauricio, Vincent Crawford, William Borke, Jason Petrakian, Chistopher Bilak, Anthony Scalia, and Brian Baranok were all sworn in during ceremonies Wednesday in the City Council chambers.
